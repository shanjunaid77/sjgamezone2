■ IMPORTANT DOWNLOAD RULES

1. Handle Shrink Links

We use link shorteners (shrinks) to keep the servers alive. Please wait for the timer and click "Get Link" carefully.

2. Close Pop-up Ads

If you see any ads, simply close them immediately. Do not click on anything inside the ad window.

3. Manage New Tabs

If a new tab opens automatically, close it and return to the original page to continue your download.
