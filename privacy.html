Privacy Protocol

Status: Active Security Layer // 2025

// 01. Data Collection

SJ Game Zone does not require user registration or accounts. We do not collect names, emails, or personal addresses. Our repository is open-access and designed for privacy-first browsing.

// 02. Connection Logs

Our hosting provider (Vercel) may log standard technical data such as your IP address and browser type for security purposes and to prevent DDoS attacks. This data is never sold or used for marketing.

// 03. External Links

When you click a link to **initiate download**, you leave SJ Game Zone. Third-party sites (like Mediafire, Mega, or ouo.io) have their own privacy protocols. We encourage users to review the privacy settings of any external host they visit.

// 04. Cookies

SJ Game Zone uses minimal cookies only to remember your search preferences and site performance. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

// 05. Security Protocol

We use SSL encryption (HTTPS) to ensure that your connection to our digital index is secure. Your browsing habits within the SJ Zone remain private.
